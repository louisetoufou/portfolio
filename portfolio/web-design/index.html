Web design

Here are a few examples of web projects I designed in house or while working for digital agencies. The following concepts were all created with short deadlines to either support client pitches or drive sales for small campaigns.

Blu-Ray landing page prototype

I worked on this project while freelancing for the London based digital agency Brands2Life. The brief was to mock up a new landing page for the Blu-Ray website that would be mobile-friendly and more interactive.

My concept was to make the website more engaging and attractive with a full-width video displaying the latest trailers, a quick review of the recent releases and a blog section for the latest news about Blu-Ray.
