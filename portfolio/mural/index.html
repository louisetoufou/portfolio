Office mural

Commissioned wall doodle for a digital agency

I got commissioned by the creative director of the London-based digital agency Mediablaze to create a mural for their office.

The brief was to create a big doodle illustration of London while incorporating branding elements of the agency. They got inspired by another doodle found in my portfolio and wanted something a bit similar. They gave me free rein to fill the doodle with lots of quirky illustrations which was very fun!
