Android app design

DAE 68 is an Android application allowing users to find the closest defibrillators in one click. This app is was developed in collaboration with an association surveilling and listing the AEDs located in Haut-Rhin (French department). I did the UX, the UI and the drawer illustration.

App screens

Based on the Material Design principles, the concept for DAE 68 was to create a clear and straightforward experience to help users find the nearest defibrillator as quickly as possible. The app first shows where the defibrillators are located in the area (1st screen). The user can then identify the closest defibrillator by clicking on the floating action button and display the itinerary via Google Maps (2nd screen). The drawer gives access to informational content on steps to take in case of emergency (4th screen).
